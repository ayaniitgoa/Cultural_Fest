ABOUT

IIT Goa, into its only 4th year of existence has already grown to be a much sought-after institution for engineering students and a melting-pot of ideas. Being young comes with its own unique brand of enthusiasm and ingenuity.

We are nestled into the vast and beautiful expanse of GEC campus in Farmagudi, Goa with a vibrant population of over three thousand students and five hundred faculty members and staff.

While we strive to achieve excellence in academic and technical endeavors, we are certainly touched by the rich culture of Goa which we want to celebrate through the first ever techno-cultural fest of IIT Goa - CultRang
